What is a moral theory?

Ethical/Moral theories are a way to determine what is it that makes an action good or not. In Business Ethics we typically invoke one or more ethical theories to clarify what is right and wrong in a particular situation.

  • The Teleological theories state that the correctness of an action is based by what they achieve, the number of good outcomes.

  • The Deontological theories determine the goodness of an action by the inherent value of the principles they follow, their intentions.
