Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

TL;DR: NO DATA IS COLLECTED!

Neither the Philosophito Android app nor the philosophito.com website collects, stores, or transmits any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Philosophito ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in relation to our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. No Data Collection

Philosophito does not collect, use, store, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • No personal information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information
  • No usage data: We do not track how you use the app or website
  • No device information: We do not collect device IDs, IP addresses, or technical information about your device
  • No location data: We do not access or collect your geographic location
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics services that track user behavior
  • No cookies: Our website does not use tracking cookies for data collection purposes

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Social Media Integration

The Service may include links to our social media pages (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube) and may include social media plugins (such as Facebook Messenger chat). These third-party services have their own privacy policies:

  • When you interact with these plugins or visit our social media pages, those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies
  • We do not control and are not responsible for the data collection practices of these third-party services
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these platforms

3.2 External Links

The Service may contain links to external websites (such as Google Play Store). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. Content Storage

All educational content in the Philosophito app is stored locally on your device. No content, notes, or quiz responses are transmitted to our servers or any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is designed for college-level students and is generally appropriate for users aged 18 and above. Since we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect information from users of any age.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or compromised through our Service.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete privacy when using our Service.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. If we ever decide to collect data in the future, we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy with detailed information about what data is collected and how it's used
  • Notify users through the app and website
  • Obtain appropriate consent where required by law

9. International Users

The Service can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Since we collect no data, there are no data transfer or international privacy law concerns.

10. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • Other applicable data protection laws worldwide

Our no-data-collection approach ensures compliance with the strictest privacy regulations.
